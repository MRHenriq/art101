Task 1: Pseudocode an everyday task - Making a ginger bug: Free bubbly ginger soda!

    Sterilize a large glass jar with boiling water
  1. Let cool
    Add ingredients
  1. Grate ¼ cup of ginger with the skin on
  2. Add ginger and ¼ cup of sugar and the ginger to the jar
  3. Add 2 cups of water and stir
    Store and feed the ginger bug
  1. Cover jar with a paper towel and a rubber band
  2. Put in fridge
  3. Wait 24 hrs and add 1tsp sugar, stir
  4. Wait 4-6 days and add more sugar, stir
    Using the ginger bug
  1. When the bug forms bubbles, pour, or strain the ginger bug liquid into a glass and add water and simple syrup or agave as desired to taste, and enjoy!
    Maintaining the ginger bug
  1. Add and stir in more ginger, sugar, and water back to the jar after use to keep the ginger bug alive!
    Alternative methods
  1. The method above can be substituted with fresh turmeric intead of ginger
  2. You can also make wild fermented sodas with juniper berries, spruce tips, and white pine needles!
